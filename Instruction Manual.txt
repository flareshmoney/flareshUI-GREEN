-=flaresh-UI Instruction Manual

Last updated: flaresh-UI Version 2.62a

-=Includes:
Github download issue fix.
Fixing missing fonts.
Changing Spectator UI's.
Changing to Medic uber tracker.
Changing Uber label when looking at Medics / getting healed by Medics.

-=Github download issue fix.
1. Download the .zip file from github.
2. Open "flareshUI-main.zip"
3. Open "flareshUI-main" in the .zip file.
4. Put "flareshui" in the custom folder.
5. Restart your game.
6. Enjoy.

-=Fixing missing fonts.
1. Go to the "flareshUI/resource/fonts" folder.
2. Select all font's in the fonts folder.
3. Download all selected fonts.
4. Restart your game.
5. Enjoy.


-=Changing Spectator UI's
1. Go to the "flareshUI/Customizations" folder.
2. Open the "SpectatorTournament.res" file.
3. Remove the "//" to the one you want to use.
4. Add "//" to the one you don't want to use.
5. Save the file and enter "Hud_reloadscheme" in console.
6. Enjoy.

-=Chaning to Medic uber tracker.
1. Go to the "flareshUI/Customizations" folder.
2. Open the "HudMedicCharge" file.
3. Remove the "//" to the one you want to use.
4. Add "//" to the one you don't want to use.
5. Copy "HudAnimations_flareshUI - Uber Tracker" To "flareshUI/Scripts"
6. Remove "HudAnimations_flareshUI" (In the scripts folder).
7. Rename "HudAnimations_flareshUI - Uber Tracker" to "HudAnimations_flareshUI".
8. Restart your game.
9. Press "C" twice.
10. Enjoy.

-=Changing Uber label when looking at Medics / getting healed by Medics.
1. Go to the "flareshUI/resource" folder.
2. Remove the "chat_english" TXT file.
3. Restart your game.
4. Enjoy.
